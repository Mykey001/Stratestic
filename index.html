Monte Carlo Engine

Monte Carlo reshuffling is computationally cheap because indicator calculations have already been performed. Each simulation only needs to shuffle the trade sequence and recompute the resulting equity curve, making the complexity roughly O(n) per simulation. With 20 trades and 1,000 simulations, the workload is tiny for modern JavaScript engines and executes almost instantly in the browser.
